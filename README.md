# Evolution-of-Cultural-Practices-in-India
A WIP writeup: How has the culture been used, as a weapon, over a century, to fulfil specific goals of those times?
The purpose is to explore the history of the various practices introduced and its evolution through various historiographies. The goal is to analyse the demographics of the two festivals Ganesh Chaturthi and Shivaji Jayanti and whether or not its significance has distorted over the years.
